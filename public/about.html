What is Naija Ipsum?

Simply put, it's a take on the lorem ipsum filler text used when doing design mockups. I was mocking up a web design one day and needed a few words (Nigerian lingo) to fill out a link. “Naija Ipsum” popped into my head, so I plugged that into my HTML. It eventually dawned on me, make at based on Nigerian Pidgin english and Nigerian delicacies.

Is there any other language available?

Simply put, pidgin is the only available language right now.

Can other delicacies be added?

Of course, other delicacies can be added with proof that they are Nigerian delicacies.
